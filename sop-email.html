Overview

The Constant Contact email is the primary distribution vehicle for the weekly market intelligence report. It contains a summary of all market data and links to the interactive dashboard, listings page, and featured article.

Important Change

Going forward, feature ONE article only (not two). The email should have a single featured article section that links to the week's content piece.

Email Structure (Top to Bottom)

The email consists of 10 sections in this exact order:

Header Section

Branded header with logo on gradient background

  • Logo: Edmund Bogen Team white logo
  • Title: "Luxury Communities Market Intelligence"
  • Subtitle: "Palm Beach County Premium Real Estate Analysis"
  • Date: "Market Data as of [Current Date]"

Featured Article Section

Single featured article with hero image

  • Hero graphic/image for the article
  • Headline in ALL CAPS, bold
  • Subheadline with context
  • 2-3 sentence description
  • CTA link: "Read our comprehensive guide"

St. Andrews Quick Link

Prominent callout to listings page

  • Text: "Listings currently at St. Andrews Country Club - click here"
  • Links to: st-andrews-listings.html
  • Large, clickable format

Executive Market Summary

Four stat boxes with key metrics

  • St. Andrews Price Range (with YoY change)
  • Total Active Listings (with change)
  • Total Sold YTD (with change)
  • Average Absorption Rate

All 21 Communities

Complete list with stats for each community

  • Community name
  • Active listings count
  • Sold YTD count
  • Absorption rate percentage
  • Color-coded borders (green = 80%+, blue = 60-79%)

Featured St. Andrews Listings

3 featured properties with details

  • Street address
  • Price (large, prominent)
  • Beds / Baths / Square Footage
  • MLS number
  • Days on Market

Mastermind Promotion

Cross-promotion for Edmund's mastermind

  • Screenshot/image of mastermind
  • Tagline about AI and business
  • Links to bogen.ai/contact

Dashboard CTA Button

Primary call-to-action button

  • Text: "View Full Interactive Dashboard"
  • Links to main dashboard (index.html)
  • Styled as prominent button

About Edmund Section

Agent bio and contact information

  • Circular headshot photo
  • Bio paragraph (Douglas Elliman affiliation)
  • Phone button: (561) 235-7575
  • Email button: edmund@bogenhomes.com

Footer

Legal and branding footer

  • Company name and report title
  • Douglas Elliman branding
  • Data source attribution
  • Office address
  • Equal Housing Opportunity disclaimer

Quality Checklist

Before sending, verify all of the following:

Pre-Send Checklist

  • Date stamp shows current week's date
  • All statistics are updated with fresh data
  • All 21 communities have current numbers
  • Featured listings are still active (not sold)
  • All links work (test each one)
  • St. Andrews listing count matches actual inventory
  • Mobile preview looks correct
  • Edmund's contact info is accurate
  • Douglas Elliman disclaimer is included
  • No typos in headlines or body text
