Overview

Featured articles are in-depth content pieces that provide value to subscribers beyond just market data. They cover topics relevant to luxury real estate buyers, such as relocation guides, market analysis, lifestyle content, and community spotlights.

File naming: newsletter-[topic-slug].html

Example: newsletter-persuasion-florida.html

Article Frequency

Articles are created as needed, not necessarily every week. When there's no new article, the email features the most recent one. New articles should be created when there's a timely topic or fresh content to share.

Article Structure Template

Every article follows this consistent structure:

1. Hero Section

Full-width header with gradient background

  • Background: Gradient from #1a3e5c to #2d5a7b
  • Main headline (compelling, benefit-focused)
  • Subtitle (context or promise)
  • Byline: "Edmund Bogen, Douglas Elliman Real Estate | [Month Year]"

2. Opening Hook

First content section that draws readers in

  • Identify the reader's problem or desire
  • Create emotional connection
  • Set up what the article will deliver
  • Keep to 2-3 paragraphs maximum

3. Data & Evidence Section

Support claims with facts and statistics

  • Financial benefits with specific numbers
  • Market statistics and trends
  • Research findings with sources cited
  • Use stat boxes for key numbers

4. Lifestyle/Benefits Section

Soft benefits beyond the numbers

  • Health and wellness benefits
  • Weather and outdoor lifestyle
  • Community and social aspects
  • Family considerations

5. Practical Strategy Section

Actionable advice readers can use

  • Step-by-step guidance
  • Tips and recommendations
  • Common mistakes to avoid
  • Timeline or process overview

6. Local Specifics

Tie back to St. Andrews and Edmund's expertise

  • St. Andrews Country Club amenities
  • Local community features
  • Comparison to other communities
  • Edmund's firsthand knowledge

7. Call-to-Action Section

Clear next steps for interested readers

  • Headline: "Ready to Explore Your Options?"
  • Phone button: (561) 235-7575
  • Email button: edmund@bogenhomes.com
  • Link back to Dashboard

Design Elements

Use these visual elements to break up content and highlight key information:

Stat Box
No State Income Tax
$20K-$37K+
Annual Savings

Use for key statistics and numbers that should stand out.

Warning Box

Important: IRS domicile rules require 183+ days in-state plus other criteria for tax benefits.

Use for caveats, disclaimers, or important warnings.

Quote Callout

"Couples who use inclusive pronouns experience greater relationship satisfaction during major life transitions."

Use for research quotes, expert citations, or key insights.

Success Box

Key Finding: 71% of grandparents took multigenerational trips in the past 3 years.

Use for positive statistics, success metrics, or benefits.

Additional Elements

  • Tables: Use for comparative data (e.g., tax rates by state, community amenities)
  • Numbered Lists: Use for step-by-step processes or ranked items
  • Bullet Lists: Use for feature lists or multiple related points
  • Section Dividers: Use cyan accent line between major sections

Writing Guidelines

Voice & Tone

  • Professional but approachable - Expert knowledge, friendly delivery
  • Data-driven - Back claims with statistics and research
  • Helpful - Focus on reader value, not sales pitch
  • Local expertise - Showcase Edmund's community knowledge

Headlines

  • Benefit-focused (what will the reader gain?)
  • Specific when possible (numbers, outcomes)
  • Active voice
  • Avoid clickbait or overpromising

Content Quality

  • Always cite data sources
  • Use recent statistics (current year preferred)
  • Include local/regional specifics
  • Proofread carefully before publishing

Research Sources

  • U.S. Census Bureau
  • Florida Realtors reports
  • Tax Foundation data
  • Academic research (psychology, economics)
  • Industry surveys (NAR, travel industry)
  • Local government data

Publishing Workflow

Step 1: Topic Selection

Choose topic based on:

  • Timeliness (seasonal, news-related)
  • Subscriber questions/interests
  • SEO opportunity
  • Edmund's expertise areas

Step 2: Research & Outline

  • Gather data and sources
  • Create section outline
  • Identify stat boxes and callouts
  • Draft headline options

Step 3: Write Content

  • Follow template structure
  • Include all design elements
  • Add source citations
  • Write CTA section

Step 4: Create Hero Graphic

  • Eye-catching image relevant to topic
  • Brand colors and styling
  • Save as [topic]-hero.png

Step 5: Build HTML Page

  • Use existing article as template
  • Update all content sections
  • Test all links
  • Check mobile responsiveness

Step 6: Publish

  • Push to GitHub
  • Verify live page loads correctly
  • Update email to reference new article
