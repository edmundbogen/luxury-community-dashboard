St. Andrews Country Club represents one of the most valuable unincorporated communities in Palm Beach County. How This Started: The May 2025 Council Directive

According to city records, Boca Raton's interest in St. Andrews annexation was formalized during a May 8, 2025 strategic planning workshop. Council members directed city staff to study potential annexations of two specific communities: St. Andrews Country Club and Le Lac, an exclusive enclave of estate lots that Forbes Magazine has called one of "America's Millionaire Capitals."

What the City Council Requested

  • Update prior annexation financial models for both communities

The Legal Process: How Florida Annexation Actually Works

Under Florida Statute Chapter 171 (the Municipal Annexation or Contraction Act), municipal annexation follows a specific, voter-driven legal framework. Here's exactly what the law requires:

Florida Statute 171.0413 ‚Äî Key Requirements

  • Contiguity: The area must be contiguous to existing city boundaries
  • Public Hearings: The city must hold at least two advertised public hearings before adopting an annexation ordinance
  • Referendum Required: A referendum must be held where registered voters in the annexation area vote
  • Majority Rule: A majority vote in favor is required for annexation to proceed
  • 2-Year Cooling Period: If voters reject annexation, the area cannot be proposed again for 2 years
  • No Forced Annexation: The city cannot impose annexation unilaterally‚Äîresident voters decide

Impact on Property Values

As a real estate professional specializing in St. Andrews and luxury South Florida properties, I'm frequently asked whether annexation would help or hurt property values. The honest answer: it depends on execution and perception.

Property Value Considerations

  • Address Prestige: A "City of Boca Raton" municipal address may carry different weight than an unincorporated Palm Beach County address with some buyers
  • Tax Implications: Lower taxes generally support values, but buyers evaluate total cost of ownership‚Äîverify the actual tax impact
  • Service Quality: Enhanced services could be a selling point‚Äîif they materialize and are measurably better
  • Uncertainty Period: Any major change can create short-term market uncertainty until the outcome is clear
  • Comparable Sales: St. Andrews already commands premium prices based on the community itself, not its municipal status
